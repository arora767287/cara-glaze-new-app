Your Questions About Peppr AI

Who’s eligible to join Peppr AI?

Peppr AI welcomes all creators of human-made content. Whether you're an artist, writer, photographer, designer, musician, or any other type of creator, Peppr AI is here to protect your work and connect you with an audience that values authentic, AI-free creativity.

How does Peppr AI protect my work?

We use encryption, watermarks, and active blockers to prevent unauthorized AI scraping or replication of your content. Peppr AI ensures your intellectual property is safe.

Can I sell my creations on Peppr AI?

Yes! You can upload your work, set your price, and earn directly from Peppr AI’s platform. Monetize your creativity seamlessly.

What is Peppr AI’s privacy promise?

We never sell your data or allow anyone to use your work without permission. Peppr AI prioritizes transparency and user trust.

How do I earn commissions?

Earn commissions when your work is purchased directly on Peppr AI or through partner platforms like Minted.

What happens if my work is misused?

Use Peppr AI’s reporting and tracking tools to address unauthorized usage. We assist in protecting your rights and ensuring accountability.
